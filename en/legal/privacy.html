3. Server Log Files and Retention Policy

Hosting Provider: This website is hosted by GitHub Inc. (88 Colin P. Kelly Jr. Street, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA)

Data Processing and Logs: When you visit our website, the hosting provider automatically collects information in the context of so-called "Server Log Files." This data is technically necessary and includes:

  • IP address
  • Date and time of the server request
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system used
  • Referrer URL (previously visited website)

Purpose: To ensure the technical security, stability, and reliable provision of our website (e.g., to detect and prevent DDoS attacks).

Data Transfer to the USA: Since GitHub is a US-based company, data (such as your IP address) may be processsed on servers located in the United States. We have concluded a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with GitHub. Furthermore, the data transfer is safeguarded by the EU-US Data Privacy Framework (DPF), to which GitHub (as a subsidiary of Microsoft) adheres, or strictly via Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), combined with technical security measures such as robust TLS/SSL encryption for all data transmission, to ensure GDPR compliance.

Legal Basis: Our overriding legitimate interest (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR) in the technical security and smooth operation of the site.

Retention and Deletion: We do not have direct access to these log files. However, GitHub may retain these logs for a limited period for security and incident-investigation purposes in accordance with their privacy policy. For more details, please refer to the GitHub Privacy Statement.

Processing Purpose:

5.1. Transfer to a Third Country (USA) and Safeguards

Your data (including IP address, name, email address, message) is transferred to Formspree’s servers in the USA. The USA is a third country without an adequate level of data protection according to EU standards.        

Risk: The USA is assessed a a third country. While the EU-US Data Privacy Framework (DPF) exists, transfers to non-certified US companies may carry the risk that your data could be subject to access by US authorities (e.g., under FISA 702) without effective legal remedies being available to you.        

Protection Measures: To ensure appropriate safeguards, we rely on the implementation of Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) issued by the EU Commission, combined with additional technical and organizational measures, as agreed with Formspree, Inc.        

Legal Basis for Transfer: By submitting the form and explicitly checking the consent box, you expressly consent to this data transfer (including the transfer of your IP address) despite the identified risks (Art. 49 Para. 1 lit. a GDPR).        

Right to Erasure: You have the right to request the deletion of data submitted via the contact form at any time. To exercise this right, please contact the Controller directly via email: contact@hanaenergy.at.        

Further information on data processing and privacy protection by Formspree can be found in their privacy policy: Formspree's Privacy Policy.
