When you visit our website, the hosting provider automatically collects information in the context of so-called "Server Log Files." This data is technically necessary and includes:

Purpose: To ensure the technical security, stability, and reliable provision of our website.

Legal Basis: Our overriding legitimate interest (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR) in the technical security and smooth operation of the site.

While GitHub does not publicly disclose a fixed retention period for all IP logs, certain security-related logs (such as Git events) may be retained for up to 7 days, and other logs may be kept longer for security and incident-investigation purposes.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies. We categorize the cookies we use as follows, in accordance with the ePrivacy Directive (TKG 2021):

4.1. Necessary Cookies (Technical Functionality)

These cookies are essential for the basic function of the website (e.g., storing your cookie consent status). They are processed based on Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest). Your prior consent is not required.

4.2. Non-Essential Cookies (Statistical/Marketing)

These cookies are used for statistical analysis or marketing purposes. These are only set and processed if you provide your explicit consent via our Cookie Consent Tool (Opt-in). The legal basis is your Consent (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. a GDPR).

Consent Documentation: Your consent status and the time of consent are logged by our system for compliance purposes (Art. 7 Para. 1 GDPR).

Withdrawal of Consent: You have the right to revoke your consent at any time, effective for the future, via the "Change Cookie Settings" link in the footer of the website. Revoking consent will stop future tracking and associated non-essential data processing.

Processing Purpose: Processing your inquiry for pre-contractual measures (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. b GDPR).

5.1. Transfer to a Third Country (USA) and Safeguards

Your data (name, email address, message) is transferred to Formspree’s servers in the USA. The USA is a third country without an adequate level of data protection according to EU standards.

This transfer carries the risk that your data could be subject to access by US authorities (e.g., under FISA 702) without effective legal remedies being available to you.

Protection Measures: To ensure appropriate safeguards, we rely on the implementation of Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) issued by the EU Commission, combined with additional technical and organizational measures, as agreed with Formspree, Inc.

Legal Basis for Transfer: By submitting the form and explicitly checking the consent box, you expressly consent to this data transfer despite the identified risks (Art. 49 Para. 1 lit. a GDPR).

Further information on data processing and privacy protection by Formspree can be found in their privacy policy: https://formspree.io/legal/privacy.
