Our website uses so-called cookies. These are small text files that are stored on your device using the browser. They serve to make our offer user-friendly. We categorize the cookies we use as follows:

4.1. Necessary Cookies (Technical Functionality)

These cookies are essential for the basic function of the website (e.g., storing the current session or your chosen language). **They are processed based on Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest) as the website cannot function correctly without them. Your prior consent is not required.**

4.2. Non-Essential Cookies (Statistical/Marketing)

**These cookies are used for statistical analysis, market research, or personalized content (e.g., Google Analytics). These cookies are only used if you provide your explicit consent via our Cookie Consent Tool (Opt-in). The legal basis is your consent (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. a GDPR and § 96 Para. 3 TKG / TTDSG).**

**You have the right to revoke your consent at any time, effective for the future, via the "Change Cookie Settings" link in the footer of the website.** Deactivating these cookies does not affect the basic functionality of the website.
