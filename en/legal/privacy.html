**Purpose:** To ensure the technical security, stability, and reliable provision of our website.

**Legal Basis:** Our overriding **legitimate interest** (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR) in the technical security and smooth operation of the site.

**Retention and Deletion:** Server log files are stored for a maximum period of **7 days** to ensure system stability and security. They are then automatically deleted unless required for evidence purposes (e.g., in the event of a cyber-attack or legal proceedings).

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies. We categorize the cookies we use as follows, in accordance with the ePrivacy Directive (TKG 2021):

4.1. Necessary Cookies (Technical Functionality)

These cookies are essential for the basic function of the website (e.g., storing your cookie consent status). They are processed based on **Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR** (legitimate interest) as the website cannot function correctly without them. Your prior consent is not required.

4.2. Non-Essential Cookies (Statistical/Marketing)

These cookies are used for statistical analysis (e.g., Google Analytics) or marketing purposes. These are only set and processed if you provide your **explicit consent** via our Cookie Consent Tool (Opt-in). The legal basis is your **Consent** (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. a GDPR).

**Consent Documentation:** Your consent status and the time of consent are logged by our system for compliance purposes (Art. 7 Para. 1 GDPR).

**Withdrawal of Consent:** You have the right to revoke your consent at any time, effective for the future, via the "**Change Cookie Settings**" link in the footer of the website. Revoking consent will stop future tracking and associated non-essential data processing.

**Protection Measures:** To ensure appropriate safeguards, we rely on the implementation of **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)** issued by the EU Commission, combined with additional technical and organizational measures, as agreed with Formspree, Inc.
