We transform buildings into power plants. Bridging the gap between Architectural Aesthetics and Energy Efficiency. We provide advanced BIPV facades and grid-free mobile energy solutions for forward-thinking projects in the DACH region.

Our Core Solutions

1. Architectural Solar (BIPV & Facade)

  • Beyond Standard Panels: Solar Cladding that mimics stone, brick, and terracotta textures.
  • Special Applications: Lightweight & flexible modules for historic buildings, curved roofs, and low-load structures.
  • Spec-in Service: We work directly with architects from the design phase to integrate energy generation without compromising aesthetics.

2. Mobile Energy Infrastructure (Grid-Free Power)

  • Mobile ESS & EV Charging: High-capacity mobile energy storage (up to 184kWh) combined with fast charging.
  • Zero Construction: Deploy high-power infrastructure immediately without expensive grid upgrades or excavation.
  • Resilience: Perfect for old buildings with limited power capacity and emergency backup needs.

3. Project Support for Green Buildings

  • Product Application: Selecting the right materials to meet Cradle to Cradle (C2C) and ESG requirements.
  • Technical Data: Providing detailed datasheets, fire safety reports (EN 13501), and installation manuals for architects.
  • Sales Coordination: Managing the supply chain from specification to delivery on site.
